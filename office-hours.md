I'm a 2026 candidate for [Blood Cancer United's Visionary of the Year](https://pages.lls.org/voy/va/va26/cbusse) campaign. As part of this campaign, I'm offering **Q2 office hours** — one-on-one sessions to help you get more out of AI-assisted development.

## What You Get

For a **minimum donation of $100** to my Visionary of the Year campaign, you'll receive:

- **One hour** of live, one-on-one assistance
- Help with **Claude Code**, **Claude Cowork** (in Cursor, Windsurf, VS Code, or JetBrains), or other AI coding tools
- Guidance on agentic workflows, prompt structuring, multi-agent patterns, or whatever you're working on
- Flexible scheduling throughout **Q2 2026** (April–June)

## How It Works

1. **Donate** — Make a donation of $100 or more at my campaign page:<br>
   [**pages.lls.org/voy/va/va26/cbusse**](https://pages.lls.org/voy/va/va26/cbusse)
2. **Email me** — Send your donation confirmation to [cbusse@singlestoneconsulting.com](mailto:cbusse@singlestoneconsulting.com) with "Office Hours" in the subject line
3. **Schedule** — I'll reply with a calendar link to book your session

## About the Campaign

Blood Cancer United's Visionary of the Year campaign brings together leaders committed to funding cures and improving the lives of patients with blood cancers. Every dollar raised goes directly to Blood Cancer United's mission — funding research, providing patient support, and advocating for policies that improve access to care.
